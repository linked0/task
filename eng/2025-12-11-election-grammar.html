English Correction: Election Terminology

Who will win the election in Gyeonggi Province Governor
Who will win the Gyeonggi Province Governor election?

Why?

"Gyeonggi Province Governor" is a Job Title (Position), not a Place.

  • You have an election IN a place (e.g., in Gyeonggi Province).
  • You have an election FOR a position (e.g., for Governor).
  • Or you combine them into a compound noun: "The [Place] [Position] election".

Other Natural Variations:

  • "Who will win the election for Gyeonggi Province Governor?" (Focus on the position)
  • "Who will be elected as the Governor of Gyeonggi Province?" (Focus on the person)
  • "Who will win the Gyeonggi gubernatorial election?" (Advanced/Formal term for Governor)
